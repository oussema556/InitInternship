IT Departement

The IT Departement is responsible for providing all the necessary IT resources to offer personalised assistance to users and guarantee the security of the cluster's information system on the one hand, and to offer telecommunication services to the cluster's customers implemented in the cluster's industrial areas on the other

The missions of the IT Departement are:

MISSION 1

Implementing the company's IT strategy.

MISSION 2

Administer and operate administrative and common servers.

MISSION 3

Maintain the computer equipment, plan the installation, configuration and troubleshooting of equipment.

MISSION 4

Manage directory servers and provide digital services to users (e-mail, wireless network, etc.)

MISSION 5

Implement a data backup and archiving policy
